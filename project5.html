Sound Design

Tool(s): Adobe Premier, stock sounds, video template

As a class we were given 4 videos to choose from, one of which we were given the task of adding sound to. I picked the same video that was used in the demonstration because it allowed the opportunity for a different take on the sounds for the same video. I used free stock sound effects and used Adobe Premier to edit them over the videos.
