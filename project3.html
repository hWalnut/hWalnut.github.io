New Species

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

I used Photoshop to combine a horse, a gorilla, and a parrot, then edit the result into a field. I color corrected the gorilla's head and the parrot's wings to match the brown of the horse. The background I used was extended vertically using generative fill, and I erased some sections of the animal's hooves to give the illusion of grass in the foreground.
