Glitch Art Collage

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

I made this collage by compositing a couple of photos I took of a beach from my school's Dominican Republic trip. Even though they appear to be the same photo in different lighting, they are actually in different spots - the orange lighting comes from a bonfire we were having. I manually overlayed both images over each other in a pattern, then mirrored them vertically and horizontally for a kaleidoscope effect.
